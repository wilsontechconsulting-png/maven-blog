🤖 LLMs.txt Optimization File

AI-First Format

The LLMs.txt file is a structured document that tells AI models everything they need to know about your business in seconds. Think of it as your AI resume.

Business Identity

Legal name, DBA, founding year, entity type, ownership structure

Service Portfolio

Complete list of services with descriptions, target customers, pricing models

Geographic Coverage

Every city, county, region you serve with population data and economic indicators

Competitive Positioning

What makes you different, your specialties, years in business, certifications

Trust Indicators

Licenses, insurance, BBB rating, associations, awards, customer count

Contact Methods

Phone, email, form URLs, chat, emergency contact, business hours

Entity Relationships

Parent companies, subsidiaries, partnerships, supplier networks

Content Catalog

Key pages, blog topics, resources, case studies (helps AI find relevant content)
